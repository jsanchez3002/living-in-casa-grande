## Part 1: Casa Grande Demographics Deep Dive

### 1.1 Population Breakdown

**Total Population:** 65,256 (2025 estimate)
**Growth Rate:** 3.2% annually (vs 0.4% national average)
**Median Age:** 34.2 years
**Median Household Income:** $52,340
**Median Home Value:** $215,000
**Median Rent:** $1,095/month

### 1.2 Five Distinct Communities

#### Community 1: Military (Barry M. Goldwater Air Force Base)
**Population Impact:** 15,000+ (active duty + families)

**Characteristics:**
- Constant turnover (PCS cycles every 2-4 years)
- Young adults 22-35 primarily
- High disposable income
- Need to buy/sell frequently
- Social isolation common
- Dating challenges (timeline uncertainty)
- Strong need for community connection

**Key Insights:**
- Military families spend $1.2M+ annually locally
- Base is 4th largest employer in Pinal County
- Many are new to Arizona (not familiar with area)

**Pain Points:**
- "How do I meet people when I don't know anyone?"
- "Where can I find reliable services?"
- "Dating is hard when I might move in 6 months"
- "Need to sell everything quickly when PCS orders come"

#### Community 2: ASU Polytechnic Campus
**Population Impact:** 8,000+ (students + faculty)

**Characteristics:**
- 18-24 age range primarily
- Budget-conscious
- Social, looking for activities
- Many are first-generation students
- Part-time work needs
- Limited transportation

**Key Insights:**
- Campus enrollment growing 8% annually
- Many students from Phoenix metro, want local connections
- Students spend $8,000+ annually locally

**Pain Points:**
- "Where can I find affordable food near campus?"
- "How do I meet local students vs just campus people?"
- "Need part-time jobs that fit my schedule"
- "What's there to do besides campus events?"

#### Community 3: Snowbirds (Seasonal Residents)
**Population Impact:** 10,000-15,000 (November-March)

**Characteristics:**
- 55+ age range
- From Midwest/Northeast primarily
- Homeowners or seasonal renters
- Higher disposable income
- Healthcare-focused
- Community-oriented

**Key Insights:**
- November-March population swells by 20%+
- Many are retirees with fixed incomes
- Strong interest in community activities
- Often become "permanent" residents

**Pain Points:**
- "What community events are happening while I'm here?"
- "Where can I find healthcare/services?"
- "How do I connect with both seasonal and year-round residents?"
- "Want to support local but don't know what's good"

#### Community 4: Young Families
**Population Impact:** 12,000+ households

**Characteristics:**
- 30-45 age range
- Children under 18
- Dual-income households
- Education-focused
- Busy schedules

**Key Insights:**
- #1 reason people leave CG is for better schools
- Median household size larger than average
- Heavy users of community services

**Pain Points:**
- "What activities are available for my kids?"
- "Which neighborhoods have the best schools?"
- "How do I find reliable contractors/services?"
- "Where can I find babysitters/nannies?"

#### Community 5: Long-Time Residents
**Population Impact:** 20,000+

**Characteristics:**
- Multi-generational families
- Deep community roots
- Local business owners
- Community leaders

**Key Insights:**
- Many are business owners
- Strong sense of community pride
- Want to see CG grow and improve
- Often skeptical of newcomers

**Pain Points:**
- "CG is changing too fast"
- "New businesses don't understand local culture"
- "Want to preserve what makes CG special"
- "Where are the good jobs coming from?"

**Issue:** User-generated content requires constant moderation

**Mitigation:**
- AI-assisted content flagging
- Community reporting system

- Block/report functionality

## Part 6: Technical Architecture Deep Dive

### 6.1 Firebase Schema Design

**Collections:**
```
users/          — User accounts
events/        — Community events
businesses/    — Business directory
marketplace/    — Buy/sell listings
jobs/          — Job postings
housing/       — Rental listings
deals/         — Local deals
matches/        — Dating matches
chats/         — Direct messages
```

**Security Rules:**
- Users can only edit their own profiles
- Businesses can edit their own listings
- Moderators can flag/delete any content
- Read access is public for most collections

### 6.2 API Design

**REST Endpoints:**
```
GET/POST /events          — List/create events
GET/PUT/DELETE /events/{id}
GET/POST /businesses      — List/create businesses
GET /businesses/{id}/reviews
POST /marketplace         — Create listing
GET /marketplace?category=x&location=y
POST /jobs                — Create job
GET /jobs?type=x&location=y
```

**Firebase Functions:**
- `onUserCreate` — Welcome email, set defaults
- `onEventCreate` — Send push notifications
- `onMatchCreate` — Notify both users
- `sendPushNotification` — Triggered by writes

### 6.3 Authentication

**Providers:**
- Email/password (primary)
- Apple Sign-In (required for App Store)
- Google Sign-In (optional)

**User Types:**
- Regular user (free)
- Premium user ($4.99/month)
- Business owner (verified)
- Admin (full access)
